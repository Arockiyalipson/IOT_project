Sender device will sense the CO,Humidity level , Temperature and Air quality from the respected sensors .
It will send the data to the reciver via using LoRaWAN protocol.

processor - ESP32 WDROOM
DHT 11 - humidity and temperature
MQ135 - Airquality
MQ-9 - CO level 

Sender recives land slide alert from Landslide alerting device which is mounted in wall of the mining ground.
for receving data it uses ESP-NOW protocol , which is ESP to ESP data sharing protocol
